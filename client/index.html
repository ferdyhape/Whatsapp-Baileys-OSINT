Usage
1.
Visit http://localhost:3000/scan and scan the QR code with your WhatsApp (adjust the URL if running on a different port)
2.
Send messages containing trigger words like search, find, cari, telusuri, or lihat followed by your query.
3.
Wrap your query in quotation marks for more specific results.
4.
Example: search "Jane Doe"
5.
The bot replies with results from Google Custom Search.
